⚠️ Important Emergency Procedures

  • Life-threatening emergencies: Always call 111 first, then contact FormanPacific
  • Have your policy/account number ready when calling (speeds up response)
  • Take photos of damage if safe to do so (helps with assessment)
  • Do not attempt repairs yourself if there's any safety risk
  • Keep all receipts for emergency expenses (fully reimbursable)
  • Our emergency response overrides normal approval processes
  • All emergency suppliers are pre-authorized for immediate work
  • Cost approval up to $50,000 automatic for emergencies
