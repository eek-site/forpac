Introduction

FormanPacific Limited ("FormanPacific", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service claims management platform and related services.

We comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 and adhere to the Privacy Principles set out in the Act. We are also committed to meeting international standards including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable to our operations.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Identity Information: Name, date of birth, gender, identification numbers
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone numbers, physical address, mailing address
  • Account Information: Username, password, account preferences, communication preferences
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, credit card information, payment history
  • Claims Information: Claim details, policy numbers, incident reports, photographs, documentation
  • Professional Information: Business name, ABN/NZBN, trade certifications, insurance details

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns
  • Location Information: GPS location (for emergency dispatch), IP-based location
  • Communication Data: Email opens, link clicks, communication preferences

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Insurance companies and brokers
  • Government agencies (WorkSafe, NZTA, etc.)
  • Credit reporting agencies
  • Background check providers
  • Other service providers in our network

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

Communication

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Enforce our terms and conditions

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity. We will notify you of any such change and your options regarding your information.

We Never Sell Your Personal Information

FormanPacific does not and will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based permissions
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Infrastructure: ISO 27001 certified data centers
  • Backup: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery

Organizational Measures

  • Regular security training for all staff
  • Strict confidentiality agreements
  • Limited access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify affected individuals within 72 hours
  • Report to the Privacy Commissioner as required
  • Provide information about the breach and our response
  • Offer support and guidance on protective measures
